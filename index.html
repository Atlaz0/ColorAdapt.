How ColorAdapt. Works

ColorAdapt. is a free browser extension that works by changing the tint on a website based on the user’s selected color-blindness type, which can also be easily changed to match the needs of the user. Our product also allows users to make texts more readable by changing the saturation of the website to further distinguish text from the background.
